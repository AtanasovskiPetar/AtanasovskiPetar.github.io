Info

Building a neural network model that plays football in a PyGame environment using PyTorch.

The Challenge

For the Robomac 2023 Competition challenge, we were tasked with developing the most effective football-playing algorithm within a PyGame environment, in order to win you had to outperform the algorithms of rival teams during the presentation day.

The Solution

Using the PyTorch ML Framework, we designed and trained a neural network that plays football within a PyGame environment. The network was being trained on different playing algorithms, and it also fine-tuning its skills through self-play. The outcome was great as we came victorious in the competition being the only team with this innovative approach.
